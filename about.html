About the class 关于课程

Lexington Chinese Senior English Academy (LCSEA) was founded in 2012 to help Lexington Chinese seniors learn English and understand American customs. Student volunteers prepare and teach all of the material.

Leaders: Andrew Wang and Michael Yang
